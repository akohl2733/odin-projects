Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a baked mac and cheese recipe. It's super easy to make from scratch in your own home for the holidays. The cheeses that we will use are cheddar and parmasean. The goal is to get that rich, gooey cheese in every inch of the noodles. Goes great with turkey, ham, or any barbeque you are having to end 2022 with a bang!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk (Whole)
  • Cheese (Parmasean/Cheddar)
  • Seasonings (Salt/PepperPaprika)
  • Bread Crumbs

Instructions

  1. Boil the Noodles - Boil in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and prepare for baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce - Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. Make the Topping - Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the Mac and Cheese - Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown
