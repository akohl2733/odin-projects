Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a baked mac and cheese recipe. It's super easy to make from scratch in your own home for the holidays. The cheeses that we will use are cheddar and parmasean. The goal is to get that rich, gooey cheese in every inch of the noodles. Goes great with turkey, ham, or any barbeque you are having to end 2022 with a bang!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk (Whole)
  • Cheese (Parmasean/Cheddar)
  • Seasonings (Salt/PepperPaprika)
  • Bread Crumbs

Instructions

  1. Boil the Noodles - Boil in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and prepare for baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce - Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. Make the Topping - Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the Mac and Cheese - Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown
Fresh Cranberry Sauce

Description

This is a damn necessity for your Thanksgiving this year. Has the best trait of any Thanksgiving dish - ease to make. This bad boy only needs three ingredients to keep you focused on not overcooking your Turkey while making your relatives think you went to culinary school.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Water
  • 1 cup of Sugar
  • 1 package of Fresh Cranberries

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil in saucepan; add sugar and cook until sugar is dissolved (about 5 minutes>
  2. Stir cranberries into the saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, and simmer until cranberries have popped and sauce is chunky, about 10 minutes or longer for desired consistency. The longer you cook it, the less chunky it will be.
  3. Pour sauce into a serving dish or jars and cover until ready to use; the pectin in the cranberries will make the cranberry sauce gel as it cools.
  4. Mix cranberry sauce with a fork before serving
Mashed Potatoes

Description

These mashed potatoes are too fattening to have on a regular basis. I only make these on holidays, and everyone wonders what's in them that's makes them so rich.

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs of Red Potatoes (peeled and quartered
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Place potatoes in a large pot with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, and cook until fork tender, about 20 minutes. Drain, add butter, and mash using a potato masher, ricer or mixer. Gradually beat in the heavy cream. Serve hot!
